Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Data Controller

The data controller for this app is Kirmes. For privacy-related inquiries, you can contact us at fw@kirmes.app.

1. Data Collection

We collect and process the following data:

  • Authentication information via Google/Apple Sign-In
  • Game progress and virtual winnings
  • Device information and game usage statistics

2. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Your consent (e.g., for analytics)
  • Performance of a contract (e.g., providing the game service)
  • Compliance with legal obligations

3. Data Usage

Your data is used to:

  • Provide and maintain the game service
  • Track game progress and virtual winnings
  • Improve user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Storage

All data is stored on secure servers within the European Union, in compliance with GDPR requirements.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law. Typically, this means:

  • Game progress data: Retained for the duration of your account
  • Analytics data: Retained for 12 months

6. Data Sharing

We do not share personal data with third parties except:

  • When required by law
  • With service providers who assist in operating our service
  • For analytics purposes (in anonymized form)

7. International Data Transfers

Your data is stored within the European Union. If we transfer data outside the EU/EEA, we will ensure adequate safeguards are in place, such as Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs).

8. User Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request data correction or deletion
  • Withdraw consent
  • Request data portability

9. Data Protection

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access or processing.

10. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for users under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal data, we will take steps to delete such information.

11. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, contact us at: fw@kirmes.app
